07 — Rules

Brand Guidelines

Correct usage and what to avoid — for designers, developers, and anyone working with the NOIR identity.

Do
✓Use full lockup (mark + wordmark) in primary contexts
✓Indigo #6366F1 on void-dark — contrast 8.9:1 AAA
✓Indigo #4F46E5 on white — contrast 7.4:1 AAA
✓Scale 3 circles proportionally, keep offset ratios
✓Font Inter for all UI text — clean and system-grade
✓Solid/filled version for ≤ 20px (favicon, notification)
✓App icon: Indigo BG + white stroke, border-radius 22%
✓Tagline: "Build Better. Scale Further." — period, not comma
Don't
✕Indigo #6366F1 on white → contrast fail (2.8:1)
✕Alter the 3-circle ratios or offset positions
✕Add drop shadow, glow, or extra effects to orbital mark
✕Stroke version at 16px — use solid/monogram instead
✕Place logo on busy backgrounds without clear space
✕Rotate, stretch, or skew the mark in any way
✕Use "NOIR" in lowercase — always uppercase
✕Use light indigo (#818CF8) on light backgrounds — fails AA
